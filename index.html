What I like about My Church

Why is it so great?

images

Here are a couple of reasons why I like my Church!

My first reason

We have many great role models who live a very Godly life and they show how to be great and kind people

My second reason

We have great food on Sundays, like rice and peas, stew pork, jerk chicken and Jamican patties!

My third reason

My church always has events that's going on, so you will never be bored. You will always have ways to worship God.

My fourth reason

I feel like my church is one big family with parents, uncles, cousins and big brothers.

My fifth reason

I feel like there is always a place for you in church, no matter what walk of life you're from.

Those are the 5 reasons why I love my church. It brings me a lot of joy to be part of a tight-knit community. What shows love to all of their members.It makes me feel loved and cared for. I hope you like my website
